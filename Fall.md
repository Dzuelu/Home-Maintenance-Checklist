## 🍂 Fall (September – November)

### Roof & Exterior
- [ ] Inspect roof and flashing before winter
- [ ] Clean gutters and downspouts (repeat after leaves finish falling)
- [ ] Install gutter guards if not already present
- [ ] Inspect chimney cap, crown, and mortar joints
- [ ] Check exterior caulking and sealants; re-apply before cold weather
- [ ] Inspect and repair siding, trim, and fascia
- [ ] Inspect and seal concrete cracks in driveway, walkways, and patio
- [ ] Clean and store exterior window screens
- [ ] Inspect storm windows and install if applicable

### Foundation & Structure
- [ ] Ensure grading still directs water away from foundation
- [ ] Check insulation in attic, basement, and crawl spaces; add if needed
- [ ] Seal gaps around pipes, cables, wires, and vents entering the house
- [ ] Inspect crawl space vapor barrier
- [ ] Check for drafts around electrical outlets on exterior walls

### HVAC & Ventilation
- [ ] Schedule professional furnace/heating system tune-up
- [ ] Replace HVAC filters
- [ ] Inspect and clean fireplace and chimney; schedule chimney sweep
- [ ] Test fireplace damper operation
- [ ] Check flue for blockages or animal nests
- [ ] Inspect and clean humidifier (if attached to HVAC)
- [ ] Reverse ceiling fan direction (clockwise to push warm air down)
- [ ] Bleed air from hot water radiators (if applicable)
- [ ] Inspect baseboard heaters and clear obstructions
- [ ] Check and clean range hood and exhaust fan filters

### Plumbing
- [ ] Drain and winterize outdoor faucets (install hose bib covers)
- [ ] Disconnect, drain, and store garden hoses
- [ ] Winterize irrigation/sprinkler system (blow out lines)
- [ ] Insulate exposed pipes in unheated areas (garage, crawl space, attic)
- [ ] Flush water heater and inspect anode rod
- [ ] Check water heater for signs of rust or leakage
- [ ] Inspect sump pump and backup battery
- [ ] Pour water into infrequently used drains to maintain trap seals
- [ ] Inspect septic system; schedule pumping if due (every 3–5 years)

### Electrical & Safety
- [ ] Test all smoke and carbon monoxide detectors; replace batteries
- [ ] Test GFCI and AFCI outlets
- [ ] Check fire extinguishers
- [ ] Review and update home emergency plan and supplies
- [ ] Test backup generator; stockpile fuel
- [ ] Check outdoor lighting for darker evenings

### Doors, Windows & Interior
- [ ] Caulk and weatherstrip all doors and windows
- [ ] Check door sweeps and thresholds for gaps
- [ ] Inspect and replace worn window glazing
- [ ] Lubricate sliding door tracks
- [ ] Check attic access door for proper insulation and sealing

### Deck, Patio & Outdoor Living
- [ ] Store or cover outdoor furniture, cushions, and umbrellas
- [ ] Winterize outdoor kitchen/grill (cover and disconnect propane)
- [ ] Drain and store above-ground pool or close in-ground pool (if applicable)
- [ ] Bring in or wrap delicate plants and garden pottery

### Landscaping & Drainage
- [ ] Aerate and overseed the lawn
- [ ] Apply fall fertilizer
- [ ] Rake leaves and remove debris from yard
- [ ] Trim perennials and remove annuals
- [ ] Mulch beds for winter protection
- [ ] Inspect retaining walls before freeze/thaw cycles
- [ ] Clean and store lawn mower (stabilize fuel or drain tank)
- [ ] Sharpen and oil garden tools before storage
- [ ] Check drainage systems for blockages

### Winter Preparation
- [ ] Stock winter supplies (ice melt, sand, snow shovels, roof rake)
- [ ] Service snow blower (oil, spark plug, shear pins)
- [ ] Inspect and organize winter gear and clothing
- [ ] Test and inspect heat tape/cables on roof and gutters (if applicable)
- [ ] Check driveway markers/reflectors
