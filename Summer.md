## ☀️ Summer (June – August)

### Roof & Exterior
- [ ] Inspect attic for proper ventilation; check for excessive heat buildup
- [ ] Check attic insulation levels and condition
- [ ] Touch up exterior paint and stain where needed
- [ ] Inspect and repair driveway and walkway cracks; seal asphalt driveway
- [ ] Check exterior caulking around windows, doors, and penetrations
- [ ] Inspect garage door weatherstripping and seals
- [ ] Inspect and maintain exterior wood (trim, shutters, sills) for rot

### HVAC & Ventilation
- [ ] Replace HVAC filters (monthly during heavy use)
- [ ] Clean A/C drain line with vinegar to prevent clogs
- [ ] Ensure A/C condensate drain is flowing freely
- [ ] Check refrigerant lines for insulation integrity
- [ ] Inspect and clean ceiling fans; tighten hardware
- [ ] Clean kitchen range hood and filter
- [ ] Check whole-house dehumidifier (if applicable)

### Plumbing
- [ ] Inspect plumbing for leaks (toilets, faucets, under sinks, water heater)
- [ ] Check toilet fill valves and flappers; replace if running
- [ ] Inspect hose bibs and outdoor faucets for drips
- [ ] Check water pressure (should be 40–60 psi)
- [ ] Inspect/clean garbage disposal
- [ ] Check for signs of hidden leaks (water stains, musty odors, high water bill)

### Electrical & Safety
- [ ] Test garage door auto-reverse and photo-eye safety features
- [ ] Inspect outdoor electrical outlets and covers
- [ ] Check landscape lighting and timer settings
- [ ] Ensure outdoor receptacles have weatherproof covers
- [ ] Test backup generator if applicable

### Interior
- [ ] Deep clean carpets, rugs, and upholstery
- [ ] Clean and organize garage, shed, and storage areas
- [ ] Inspect grout and caulk in bathrooms and kitchen; repair as needed
- [ ] Check all interior doors for proper operation and alignment
- [ ] Clean refrigerator coils (behind or underneath unit)
- [ ] Clean dishwasher filter and run a cleaning cycle
- [ ] Vacuum behind and under major appliances
- [ ] Inspect and clean light fixtures
- [ ] Flip or rotate mattresses

### Deck, Patio & Outdoor Living
- [ ] Inspect and clean outdoor grill (burners, grates, grease trap)
- [ ] Check deck stain/sealant and reapply if faded
- [ ] Inspect outdoor structures (pergola, gazebo, playset) for stability
- [ ] Check pool/spa equipment, chemical levels, and safety barriers (if applicable)

### Landscaping & Drainage
- [ ] Maintain lawn mowing schedule (proper blade height for grass type)
- [ ] Service lawn mower (sharpen blades, oil change, air filter)
- [ ] Maintain garden tools (sharpen, clean, oil)
- [ ] Check irrigation system coverage and adjust sprinkler heads
- [ ] Inspect and maintain compost bin
- [ ] Weed and edge garden beds
- [ ] Trim hedges and overgrown vegetation

### Pest Control
- [ ] Check for and treat pest infestations (ants, termites, wasps, mosquitoes)
- [ ] Remove standing water to prevent mosquito breeding
- [ ] Inspect and treat for carpenter bees/wood-boring insects
- [ ] Check basement and crawl space for moisture and pests
- [ ] Inspect window and door screens for holes (pest entry)
