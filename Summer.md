## ☀️ Summer (June – August)

### Roof & Exterior
1. Inspect attic for proper ventilation; check for excessive heat buildup
1. Check attic insulation levels and condition
1. Check exterior caulking around windows, doors, and penetrations
1. Inspect and maintain exterior wood (trim, shutters, sills) for rot
1. Inspect garage door weatherstripping and seals
1. Inspect and repair driveway and walkway cracks; seal asphalt driveway
1. Touch up exterior paint and stain where needed

### HVAC & Ventilation
1. Replace HVAC filters (monthly during heavy use)
1. Clean kitchen range hood and filter
1. Clean A/C drain line with vinegar to prevent clogs
1. Ensure A/C condensate drain is flowing freely
1. Check refrigerant lines for insulation integrity
1. Inspect and clean ceiling fans; tighten hardware
1. Check whole-house dehumidifier (if applicable)

### Plumbing
1. Check for signs of hidden leaks (water stains, musty odors, high water bill)
1. Inspect plumbing for leaks (toilets, faucets, under sinks, water heater)
1. Check water pressure (should be 40–60 psi)
1. Check toilet fill valves and flappers; replace if running
1. Inspect hose bibs and outdoor faucets for drips
1. Inspect/clean garbage disposal

### Electrical & Safety
1. Test garage door auto-reverse and photo-eye safety features
1. Ensure outdoor receptacles have weatherproof covers
1. Inspect outdoor electrical outlets and covers
1. Test backup generator if applicable
1. Check landscape lighting and timer settings

### Interior
1. Clean refrigerator coils (behind or underneath unit)
1. Inspect grout and caulk in bathrooms and kitchen; repair as needed
1. Clean dishwasher filter and run a cleaning cycle
1. Vacuum behind and under major appliances
1. Check all interior doors for proper operation and alignment
1. Inspect and clean light fixtures
1. Deep clean carpets, rugs, and upholstery
1. Clean and organize garage, shed, and storage areas
1. Flip or rotate mattresses

### Deck, Patio & Outdoor Living
1. Check pool/spa equipment, chemical levels, and safety barriers (if applicable)
1. Inspect outdoor structures (pergola, gazebo, playset) for stability
1. Inspect and clean outdoor grill (burners, grates, grease trap)
1. Check deck stain/sealant and reapply if faded

### Landscaping & Drainage
1. Service lawn mower (sharpen blades, oil change, air filter)
1. Check irrigation system coverage and adjust sprinkler heads
1. Trim hedges and overgrown vegetation
1. Maintain lawn mowing schedule (proper blade height for grass type)
1. Maintain garden tools (sharpen, clean, oil)
1. Weed and edge garden beds
1. Inspect and maintain compost bin

### Pest Control
1. Check for and treat pest infestations (ants, termites, wasps, mosquitoes)
1. Inspect and treat for carpenter bees/wood-boring insects
1. Check basement and crawl space for moisture and pests
1. Remove standing water to prevent mosquito breeding
1. Inspect window and door screens for holes (pest entry)
