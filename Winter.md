## ❄️ Winter (December – February)

### Roof & Exterior
1. Monitor icicle buildup on gutters and eaves
1. Check for ice dams on the roof; use roof rake to remove excess snow
1. Inspect attic for signs of moisture, frost, or leaks
1. Check exterior vents to ensure they are not blocked by snow/ice
1. Inspect storm doors and windows for damage

### Foundation & Structure
1. Ensure sump pump pit hasn't frozen
1. Monitor foundation for frost heave or new cracks
1. Check basement for water intrusion during thaws

### HVAC & Ventilation
1. Inspect and clean wood stove/pellet stove (if applicable)
1. Check furnace operation; listen for unusual noises
1. Ensure heating vents are unobstructed by furniture or rugs
1. Replace HVAC filters (monthly during heavy heating use)
1. Monitor humidity levels (aim for 30–50%); adjust humidifier as needed
1. Check for cold drafts near windows, outlets, and doors
1. Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans

### Plumbing
1. Monitor pipes in unheated areas for freezing; open cabinet doors during extreme cold
1. Know how to shut off main water supply in an emergency
1. Check water heater temperature (set to 120°F)
1. Inspect water heater for leaks or unusual sounds
1. Test sump pump and backup battery
1. Run water briefly in seldom-used fixtures to prevent trap dry-out
1. Check for condensation on windows (indicator of excess humidity)

### Electrical & Safety
1. Test smoke and carbon monoxide detectors (especially with heating equipment running)
1. Check fire extinguishers (ensure charged and accessible)
1. Ensure space heaters have proper clearance and are not on extension cords
1. Inspect electrical cords, outlets, and power strips for wear or overloading
1. Check holiday lighting for damaged wires; use timers to prevent overheating
1. Test GFCI and AFCI outlets

### Interior Maintenance
1. Inspect stairs, railings, and banisters for stability
1. Check under sinks for leaks or moisture
1. Inspect grout and caulk in bathrooms and kitchen; re-seal if needed
1. Check and replace worn door weatherstripping
1. Lubricate door hinges, locks, deadbolts, and garage door springs
1. Deep clean oven, stovetop, and kitchen appliances
1. Clean refrigerator coils and drip pan
1. Inspect and tighten cabinet hardware
1. Organize and declutter closets, pantry, and storage areas
1. Inspect and clean window treatments (blinds, curtains)
1. Vacuum and clean behind/under furniture

### Garage & Vehicles
1. Ensure vehicles have winter emergency kits
1. Keep garage door properly sealed to prevent cold air and pests
1. Inspect and maintain snow blower during season
1. Check garage floor for cracks and seal if needed

### Safety & Emergency Preparedness
1. Ensure carbon monoxide detectors are working near all fuel-burning appliances
1. Keep a path clear to all exits in case of emergency
1. Check emergency supplies (flashlights, batteries, water, food)
1. Review home insurance policy and update coverage if needed

### Planning
1. Plan spring projects and research contractors/get estimates
1. Review home maintenance log and update records
1. Create a budget for upcoming home improvement projects
1. Research and schedule any major spring repairs or upgrades
1. Inventory tools and supplies; note what needs replacing
