## ❄️ Winter (December – February)

### Roof & Exterior
- [ ] Check for ice dams on the roof; use roof rake to remove excess snow
- [ ] Monitor icicle buildup on gutters and eaves
- [ ] Inspect attic for signs of moisture, frost, or leaks
- [ ] Check exterior vents to ensure they are not blocked by snow/ice
- [ ] Inspect storm doors and windows for damage

### Foundation & Structure
- [ ] Monitor foundation for frost heave or new cracks
- [ ] Check basement for water intrusion during thaws
- [ ] Ensure sump pump pit hasn't frozen

### HVAC & Ventilation
- [ ] Replace HVAC filters (monthly during heavy heating use)
- [ ] Check furnace operation; listen for unusual noises
- [ ] Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans
- [ ] Monitor humidity levels (aim for 30–50%); adjust humidifier as needed
- [ ] Check for cold drafts near windows, outlets, and doors
- [ ] Inspect and clean wood stove/pellet stove (if applicable)
- [ ] Ensure heating vents are unobstructed by furniture or rugs

### Plumbing
- [ ] Monitor pipes in unheated areas for freezing; open cabinet doors during extreme cold
- [ ] Know how to shut off main water supply in an emergency
- [ ] Check water heater temperature (set to 120°F)
- [ ] Inspect water heater for leaks or unusual sounds
- [ ] Test sump pump and backup battery
- [ ] Run water briefly in seldom-used fixtures to prevent trap dry-out
- [ ] Check for condensation on windows (indicator of excess humidity)

### Electrical & Safety
- [ ] Test GFCI and AFCI outlets
- [ ] Check fire extinguishers (ensure charged and accessible)
- [ ] Test smoke and carbon monoxide detectors (especially with heating equipment running)
- [ ] Inspect electrical cords, outlets, and power strips for wear or overloading
- [ ] Check holiday lighting for damaged wires; use timers to prevent overheating
- [ ] Ensure space heaters have proper clearance and are not on extension cords

### Interior Maintenance
- [ ] Inspect grout and caulk in bathrooms and kitchen; re-seal if needed
- [ ] Deep clean oven, stovetop, and kitchen appliances
- [ ] Clean refrigerator coils and drip pan
- [ ] Lubricate door hinges, locks, deadbolts, and garage door springs
- [ ] Inspect and tighten cabinet hardware
- [ ] Check under sinks for leaks or moisture
- [ ] Organize and declutter closets, pantry, and storage areas
- [ ] Inspect and clean window treatments (blinds, curtains)
- [ ] Vacuum and clean behind/under furniture
- [ ] Check and replace worn door weatherstripping
- [ ] Inspect stairs, railings, and banisters for stability

### Garage & Vehicles
- [ ] Keep garage door properly sealed to prevent cold air and pests
- [ ] Check garage floor for cracks and seal if needed
- [ ] Ensure vehicles have winter emergency kits
- [ ] Inspect and maintain snow blower during season

### Safety & Emergency Preparedness
- [ ] Review home insurance policy and update coverage if needed
- [ ] Check emergency supplies (flashlights, batteries, water, food)
- [ ] Ensure carbon monoxide detectors are working near all fuel-burning appliances
- [ ] Keep a path clear to all exits in case of emergency

### Planning
- [ ] Plan spring projects and research contractors/get estimates
- [ ] Review home maintenance log and update records
- [ ] Create a budget for upcoming home improvement projects
- [ ] Research and schedule any major spring repairs or upgrades
- [ ] Inventory tools and supplies; note what needs replacing
