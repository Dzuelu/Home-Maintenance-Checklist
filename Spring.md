## 🌸 Spring (March – May)

### Roof & Exterior
1. Inspect roof for winter damage (missing, cracked, or curling shingles)
1. Check roof flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights
1. Check chimney cap and mortar joints
1. Clean gutters and downspouts; ensure proper drainage
1. Inspect and repair soffit and fascia
1. Check exterior siding for cracks, warping, or peeling paint
1. Inspect all exterior trim and caulking; re-caulk as needed
1. Inspect and clean skylights
1. Power wash siding, deck, driveway, and walkways
1. Inspect and repair fencing and gates

### Foundation & Structure
1. Inspect foundation for cracks, settling, or water intrusion
1. Inspect support beams, joists, and subfloor in crawl space
1. Check basement/crawl space for moisture, mold, or pests
1. Ensure grading slopes away from the foundation
1. Clear window wells of debris

### HVAC & Ventilation
1. Clean dryer vent (interior and exterior)
1. Inspect ductwork for leaks or damage
1. Schedule professional A/C tune-up and inspection
1. Replace HVAC filters
1. Clean A/C condenser coils and clear debris around outdoor unit
1. Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans
1. Check whole-house fan operation (if applicable)

### Plumbing
1. Check water heater T&P (temperature and pressure) relief valve
1. Inspect water heater anode rod; replace if heavily corroded
1. Flush water heater to remove sediment
1. Test sump pump by pouring water into the pit
1. Inspect sump pump discharge line for blockages
1. Inspect washing machine hoses for bulging or cracks
1. Inspect all visible plumbing for leaks (under sinks, toilets, water line connections)
1. Turn on outdoor faucets and check for freeze damage/leaks
1. Start up and inspect irrigation/sprinkler system
1. Snake/flush slow drains
1. Check water softener and replenish salt (if applicable)

### Electrical & Safety
1. Test all smoke and carbon monoxide detectors; replace batteries
1. Check fire extinguishers (charge level and expiration)
1. Inspect electrical panel for signs of corrosion or overheating
1. Test all GFCI and AFCI outlets
1. Test garage door auto-reverse safety feature
1. Inspect extension cords and power strips for damage
1. Check outdoor lighting and replace burned-out bulbs

### Doors, Windows & Interior
1. Inspect ceilings and walls for cracks, stains, or water damage
1. Check weatherstripping on all doors and windows
1. Clean window tracks and weep holes
1. Inspect and adjust door thresholds
1. Check window operation and hardware
1. Inspect and repair window screens
1. Lubricate door hinges, locks, and deadbolts
1. Touch up interior paint as needed

### Deck, Patio & Outdoor Living
1. Check railings for stability
1. Inspect deck/patio for loose boards, popped nails, or rot
1. Stain or seal deck if needed
1. Uncover and inspect outdoor furniture
1. Clean and inspect outdoor grill
1. Inspect and clean outdoor fans

### Landscaping & Drainage
1. Remove dead trees or hanging branches
1. Trim trees and shrubs away from the house (maintain 12" clearance)
1. Check drainage ditches, French drains, and dry wells
1. Clean and inspect patio drains
1. Inspect retaining walls for movement or damage
1. Mulch garden beds
1. Reseed bare spots and fertilize the lawn

### Pest Control
1. Check for signs of termites (mud tubes, wood damage)
1. Inspect exterior for entry points (gaps, holes, cracks) and seal them
1. Inspect attic for signs of animal intrusion
1. Schedule pest inspection/treatment if needed
