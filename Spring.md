## 🌸 Spring (March – May)

### Roof & Exterior
- [ ] Inspect roof for winter damage (missing, cracked, or curling shingles)
- [ ] Check roof flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights
- [ ] Clean gutters and downspouts; ensure proper drainage
- [ ] Inspect and repair soffit and fascia
- [ ] Check exterior siding for cracks, warping, or peeling paint
- [ ] Inspect all exterior trim and caulking; re-caulk as needed
- [ ] Power wash siding, deck, driveway, and walkways
- [ ] Inspect and repair fencing and gates
- [ ] Check chimney cap and mortar joints
- [ ] Inspect and clean skylights

### Foundation & Structure
- [ ] Inspect foundation for cracks, settling, or water intrusion
- [ ] Ensure grading slopes away from the foundation
- [ ] Check basement/crawl space for moisture, mold, or pests
- [ ] Inspect support beams, joists, and subfloor in crawl space
- [ ] Clear window wells of debris

### HVAC & Ventilation
- [ ] Schedule professional A/C tune-up and inspection
- [ ] Replace HVAC filters
- [ ] Clean A/C condenser coils and clear debris around outdoor unit
- [ ] Inspect ductwork for leaks or damage
- [ ] Clean dryer vent (interior and exterior)
- [ ] Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans
- [ ] Check whole-house fan operation (if applicable)

### Plumbing
- [ ] Flush water heater to remove sediment
- [ ] Inspect water heater anode rod; replace if heavily corroded
- [ ] Check water heater T&P (temperature and pressure) relief valve
- [ ] Inspect all visible plumbing for leaks (under sinks, toilets, water line connections)
- [ ] Turn on outdoor faucets and check for freeze damage/leaks
- [ ] Start up and inspect irrigation/sprinkler system
- [ ] Test sump pump by pouring water into the pit
- [ ] Inspect sump pump discharge line for blockages
- [ ] Snake/flush slow drains
- [ ] Check water softener and replenish salt (if applicable)
- [ ] Inspect washing machine hoses for bulging or cracks

### Electrical & Safety
- [ ] Test all smoke and carbon monoxide detectors; replace batteries
- [ ] Test all GFCI and AFCI outlets
- [ ] Check fire extinguishers (charge level and expiration)
- [ ] Inspect electrical panel for signs of corrosion or overheating
- [ ] Test garage door auto-reverse safety feature
- [ ] Check outdoor lighting and replace burned-out bulbs
- [ ] Inspect extension cords and power strips for damage

### Doors, Windows & Interior
- [ ] Inspect and repair window screens
- [ ] Check weatherstripping on all doors and windows
- [ ] Lubricate door hinges, locks, and deadbolts
- [ ] Inspect and adjust door thresholds
- [ ] Check window operation and hardware
- [ ] Touch up interior paint as needed
- [ ] Inspect ceilings and walls for cracks, stains, or water damage
- [ ] Clean window tracks and weep holes

### Deck, Patio & Outdoor Living
- [ ] Inspect deck/patio for loose boards, popped nails, or rot
- [ ] Stain or seal deck if needed
- [ ] Check railings for stability
- [ ] Uncover and inspect outdoor furniture
- [ ] Clean and inspect outdoor grill
- [ ] Inspect and clean outdoor fans

### Landscaping & Drainage
- [ ] Trim trees and shrubs away from the house (maintain 12" clearance)
- [ ] Remove dead trees or hanging branches
- [ ] Reseed bare spots and fertilize the lawn
- [ ] Mulch garden beds
- [ ] Inspect retaining walls for movement or damage
- [ ] Check drainage ditches, French drains, and dry wells
- [ ] Clean and inspect patio drains

### Pest Control
- [ ] Inspect exterior for entry points (gaps, holes, cracks) and seal them
- [ ] Check for signs of termites (mud tubes, wood damage)
- [ ] Schedule pest inspection/treatment if needed
- [ ] Inspect attic for signs of animal intrusion
